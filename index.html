Last updated: June 1, 2026

SiegeIQ is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. If you have questions, contact us at privacy@siegeiq.gg

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and a password hash. We never store plain-text passwords.

Gameplay clips: When you upload a video clip for analysis, the file is stored securely in Cloudflare R2 encrypted cloud storage. Clips are used solely to generate your coaching report and are never shared, sold, or used to train AI models.

YouTube links: If you submit a YouTube URL, we store the URL for analysis purposes only. We do not download or store the video content.

Coaching reports: Your analysis results, scores, and observations are stored in your account so you can access them later from your dashboard.

Payment information: Payments are processed by Stripe. SiegeIQ never sees, stores, or processes your card number, CVV, or billing address. Stripe handles all payment data under their own PCI-compliant systems.

Newsletter: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we store your email address to send coaching tips and product updates. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email.

Usage data: We collect basic analytics including page visits, feature usage, and error logs to improve the product. This data is aggregated and not linked to your identity.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Generate AI coaching reports from your submitted clips
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Send transactional emails (analysis complete, subscription receipts)
  • Send newsletter emails if you have opted in
  • Improve SiegeIQ's accuracy, features, and performance
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your gameplay footage to train AI models. We do not share your personal information with advertisers.

3. Data Retention

Uploaded clips are stored for 90 days after upload, then automatically deleted. Coaching reports and account data are retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

SiegeIQ uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Anthropic Claude — AI analysis engine. Clips are processed per-request and not retained by Anthropic for training.
  • Stripe — Payment processing. Stripe is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified.
  • Cloudflare R2 — Encrypted video storage.
  • Supabase — Database hosting. Data is stored in the EU-West region.
  • Resend — Transactional and newsletter email delivery.
  • ElevenLabs — AI voice synthesis for coaching audio. Text is sent per-request only.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time
  • Data portability — request a copy of your coaching reports

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@siegeiq.gg and we will respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies

SiegeIQ uses only essential cookies required for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics cookies. Your theme preference is stored in your browser's localStorage, not in a cookie.

7. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Uploaded clips are stored with encryption at rest. Passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt. We conduct regular security reviews and promptly address any vulnerabilities.

SiegeIQ is not intended for users under the age of 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this service. If we become aware that a user under 13 has created an account, we will delete it promptly.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device. SiegeIQ uses cookies and localStorage to operate the service and remember your preferences.

Cookies We Use

Authentication session cookie — Set by Clerk when you log in. Expires after 7 days of inactivity.

Stripe fraud prevention — Set by Stripe during payment processing. Stripe is PCI DSS Level 1 certified.

Theme preference (localStorage) — Stores your colour theme locally. No personal data, never leaves your device.

Review count (localStorage) — Tracks free reviews used this month. Resets monthly. Never leaves your browser.

Cookies We Do NOT Use

No advertising cookies, no Google Analytics, no Mixpanel, no social media tracking pixels, no cross-site tracking of any kind.

Managing Cookies

SiegeIQ only uses strictly necessary cookies so there are no optional cookies to configure. Blocking all cookies will prevent login. Clear localStorage via DevTools — Application — Storage — Clear Site Data.
